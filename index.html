Emma Green is a Front End Developer and artist based in Toronto who combines her creativity and technical skills to create engaging, intuitive and accessible websites. Through making, she is constantly re-imagining reality and virtuality. Obsessed with vernacular architecture and the materiality of place, she is excited by life and space online and the endless possibilities of the web.

Before entering tech, she worked in hospitality thriving in fast-paced collaborative roles, bringing her empathetic nature and curious mindset to every team. Most recently, she co-founded and is a current collective member of the plumb, an artist-run project space in Toronto that launched in the summer of 2020.
